About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Institute for Machine Learning at the ETH Zurich. My research interests broadly include machine learning, computer vision, and signal processing. Currently, my research focuses on the analysis of the robustness of classifiers and its connection with the geometry of deep networks. I received a PhD in Computer and Communication Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Pascal Frossard at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), Switzerland. During my PhD, I interned for the Apple AI Research in 2017 where I worked on autonomous technologies. I received my M.S. degree in Communication Systems from the EPFL in 2014 where I worked as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Martin Vetterli. In 2012, I got my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic), Iran.
